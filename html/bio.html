About Gregory Wisener

Gregory Wisener is an original piano composer and performer. He has been passionate about music for decades, his mission being to provide a musical reprise to the listener.
Residing in North County of Southern California, Gregory appears at markets in the area weekly. His audience comes from all walks of life, all of whom seek his romantic and calming compositions. His music is best for:
  • Busy people looking for calming music
  • Poets at heart looking for background reflective meditation music
  • The weary seeking relaxation and sleep music
  • Instrumental "lullabies" for young families with babies
  • Background study music for the stressed college student
  • Classic styled piano for aficionados of timeless, artful expression
